# HR Monthly Meeting Deck
## Polytron Board Deck Reference

---

## TUJUAN DECK

HR Monthly Meeting adalah forum bulanan untuk melaporkan kondisi organisasi dan
people kepada Top Management. Deck ini menjawab 3 pertanyaan utama management:
1. **Bagaimana kondisi talent dan organisasi kita bulan ini?**
2. **Apa yang perlu diputuskan atau didukung oleh management?**
3. **Apa risiko people yang perlu diantisipasi?**

---

## AUDIENS & TONE

- **Audiens**: CEO, C-Suite, VP (bukan hanya HR)
- **Tone**: Data-driven, candid, forward-looking
- **Hindari**: Laporan kegiatan HR — fokus pada dampak bisnis dari people metrics

---

## STRUKTUR SLIDE (8–12 Slide)

### Zona 1 — Pembuka (2 slide)

**Slide 1: Cover**
```
Background: Charcoal (#2C2C2C) — berbeda dari QBR untuk diferensiasi visual
Konten:
- Logo Polytron (putih, kiri atas)
- "HR MONTHLY MEETING" — label kecil putih, di atas judul
- "People & Organization Update" — judul besar, putih
- "[Bulan] [Tahun]" — subtitle
- "Untuk: Top Management Team" — audience label
- Tagline: "Strategy builds structure, People drive growth."
```

**Slide 2: HR Scorecard — 1 Halaman Ringkasan**
```
Header: "HR Scorecard [Bulan]: [Insight utama kondisi organisasi]"
Format: Grid 2×3 metrik utama + traffic light

Metrik:
  1. Headcount aktif vs plan
  2. Turnover rate (bulan ini) vs benchmark
  3. Time-to-fill posisi kritis
  4. Employee Engagement Score (terakhir diukur)
  5. Training completion rate
  6. Succession readiness (% posisi kritis punya suksesor)

Setiap kartu: angka besar (40pt) + label + trend indicator (↑↓→) + status dot
```

### Zona 2 — People Metrics Deep Dive (4–5 slide)

**Slide 3: Workforce Snapshot**
```
Header: "Workforce [Bulan]: Headcount [naik/turun/stabil] — [insight kunci]"
Layout: 2 kolom

Kiri (60%) — Tabel atau chart:
  - Headcount per BU: CE | EV | Operations | HR | Finance | Other
  - vs bulan lalu + vs plan
  - Trend 3 bulan terakhir (mini line chart)

Kanan (40%) — 3 stat callout:
  - Total headcount: [X] (vs plan [Y])
  - New hire bulan ini: [X]
  - Attrition bulan ini: [X]
```

**Slide 4: Turnover & Retention Analysis**
```
Header: "Turnover [Bulan]: [X]% — [di atas/di bawah/sesuai] target [Y]%"
Layout: 2 bagian

Atas — Bar chart: Turnover rate 6 bulan terakhir + target line
Bawah — Breakdown tabel:
  - Turnover by BU
  - Turnover by level (Staff / Supervisor / Manager / Leader)
  - Voluntary vs Involuntary
  - Top 3 alasan resign (dari exit interview)

Callout box: "High-performer turnover: [X]% — [status dan tindakan]"
```

**Slide 5: Recruitment Pipeline**
```
Header: "Rekrutmen [Bulan]: [X] posisi terbuka — [X] posisi kritis memerlukan perhatian"
Format: Pipeline funnel atau tabel status

Tabel posisi kritis:
  | Posisi | BU | Open Since | Status | Target Fill |
  |---|---|---|---|---|
  | [Posisi 1] | CE | [X] hari | Interview stage | [Tanggal] |
  | [Posisi 2] | EV | [X] hari | Offer stage | [Tanggal] |

Metrik:
  - Time-to-fill rata-rata: [X] hari (target: [Y] hari)
  - Offer acceptance rate: [X]%
  - Internal fill rate: [X]%
```

**Slide 6: Learning & Development Update**
```
Header: "L&D [Bulan]: [X]% completion rate — [insight tentang kapabilitas kritis]"
Format: 2 kolom

Kiri — Program summary:
  - Program berjalan bulan ini: [X]
  - Peserta terlatih: [X] orang
  - Jam pelatihan total: [X] jam/karyawan
  - Completion rate: [X]% (target: [Y]%)

Kanan — Highlight program:
  - [Program prioritas 1]: [X] peserta, [status]
  - [Program prioritas 2]: [X] peserta, [status]
  - Upcoming critical program: [nama program] — [tanggal]
```

**Slide 7: Engagement & Culture Pulse**
```
Header: "Engagement [periode pengukuran]: Score [X]% — [naik/turun/stabil] [X]ppt"

Bawah — Culture initiatives bulan ini:
  - [Inisiatif 1]: [status + dampak]
```

## CATATAN PPTX KHUSUS HR MONTHLY

- **Warna dominan**: Charcoal (#2C2C2C) untuk cover & closing, merah untuk aksen metrik
- **Engagement score**: Gunakan donut chart atau gauge, bukan bar biasa
- **Headcount chart**: Stacked bar per BU (6 bulan terakhir) untuk trend
- **Pipeline tabel**: Baris posisi kritis tanpa suksesor — highlight dengan background amber
- **Tone data label**: Turnover yang baik = merah karena turun; context matters, pastikan label jelas
- **Selalu sertakan**: Benchmark industri untuk metrik turnover dan engagement (sebagai konteks)
